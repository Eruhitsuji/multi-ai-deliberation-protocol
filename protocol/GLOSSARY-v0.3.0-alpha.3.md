# MADP v0.3.0-alpha.3 Glossary

Status: normative additions to the alpha.2 glossary.

- **Adaptive Role**: A temporary analytical function assigned for a current issue or round. It does not by itself change participant authority.
- **Assisted Conformance**: Participation in which a facilitator or mediator helps a participant consume or produce protocol-compatible material.
- **Canonicalization**: Strict conversion of validated or sufficiently clear input into a canonical artifact while preserving meaning and authority.
- **Capability Profile**: A record of what a participant can and cannot reliably read, produce, validate, or execute.
- **Claim Ledger**: A claim-level record of type, importance, provenance, verification, contradiction, and decision usability.
- **Decision Policy**: The human-defined rule that determines when a team decision is formally approved.
- **Help Assistant**: A protocol-support role without canonical state, decision, approval, or execution authority.
- **Independence Group**: Participants or role actors whose outputs are not treated as independent evidence because they share a model, chat, source, or controlling context.
- **Limited-Capability Participant**: A participant unable to consume or produce one or more canonical formats but still able to provide bounded useful input.
- **Next Action Card**: A human-facing and machine-readable instruction emitted whenever workflow progress legitimately requires user action.
- **Normalization Record**: An auditable mapping from preserved raw response text to a proposed structured interpretation.
- **Opinion-Only Participation**: Participation that may contribute ideas or critique but cannot directly modify canonical state or supply approval.
- **Plain Relay**: A concise natural-language relay intended for participants that cannot use canonical YAML, files, archives, or URLs.
- **Response Ingestion**: The process of preserving, parsing, validating, classifying, normalizing, quarantining, or rejecting a received response.
- **Session Minutes**: A reviewable human-oriented record that is distinct from canonical session state and from formal approval.
- **Team Proxy**: A participant relaying another person's statement. A proxy statement remains unconfirmed unless the represented person or an authorized process confirms it.
- **Tolerant Ingestion / Strict Canonicalization**: The principle that useful input may arrive in many formats, while only validated and auditable interpretations enter canonical records.
